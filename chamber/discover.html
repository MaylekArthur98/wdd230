Demographics

Silver Spring, Maryland, is a census-designated place (CDP) located in southeastern Montgomery County, near Washington, D.C. As of the 2020 census, it had a population of 81,015. Here are some key demographic details:

  • Population: 81,015 residents.
  • Households: 32,114 households.
  • Average annual income: $83,782.
